About me

I am a Ph.D. student in Artificial Intelligence at Korea University, conducting research on speech and audio generation. My work centers on developing efficient and high-quality models for real-world speech and audio applications.

I am particularly interested in generative modeling frameworks for speech, including Text-to-Speech, Speech Enhancement, Audio Super-Resolution, and broader multimodal or language-driven generation paradigms.

Research Interests

  • Text-to-Speech
  • Speech Enhancement
  • Audio Super-Resolution
  • Generative Models
  • Large Language Models
